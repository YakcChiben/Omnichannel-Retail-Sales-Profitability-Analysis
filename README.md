# Omnichannel-Retail-Sales-Profitability-Analysis
## Power BI Project | Sales Analytics | Business Intelligence

This project analyzes a multi-country online and in-store retail dataset to uncover revenue drivers, profitability trends, and operational performance across sales channels.

The dashboard is designed to provide decision-makers with a clear view of revenue performance, profit efficiency, order behavior, and shipping impact.

## Key Insights
High-Level KPIs

Gross Revenue: $56.40M

Net Revenue: $42.32M

Net Profit: $41.53M

Total Orders: 44.8K

## Channel Performance

Performance analyzed across Online and In-store channels

Revenue segmented by shipment provider and return status

Operational impact measured through return behavior

Shipment & Return Analysis

Major carriers (FedEx, UPS, DHL, Royal Mail) generate similar revenue volumes

Returned orders consistently contribute lower net revenue

Shipping and returns significantly influence final profitability

Quarterly Sales Trends

Strong performance in Q1 and Q2

Noticeable revenue decline toward Q4

Indicates potential seasonal or demand-cycle patterns

Payment Behavior

Revenue evenly distributed across Bank Transfer, Credit Card, and PayPal

No heavy dependency on a single payment method
